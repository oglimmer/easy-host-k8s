Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 3/30/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how oglimmer.com / Oliver Zimpasser ("we", "our", "us") processes personal data when you use easy-host (the "Platform"). It applies to all visitors and registered users.

1. Controller

Controller under Art. 4(7) GDPR:
Oliver Zimpasser, Berliner Str. 39a, 63110 Rodgau, Germany
Email: oglimmer@gmail.com

2. Data We Process

We process the following categories of data depending on how you use the Platform:

  • Account data (e.g., email address, login data via OAuth providers).
  • User content you create (e.g., uploaded files, HTML content, and related metadata).
  • Usage data (e.g., pages viewed, feature interactions).
  • Technical data (e.g., IP address, browser/user-agent, device information).

3. Purposes and Legal Bases

  • Service provision and account management (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): operating the Platform, authentication, transactional communications, storing and serving your content.
  • Security, abuse prevention and troubleshooting (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): maintaining service integrity, preventing misuse, logging limited technical data such as IP address and user-agent.
  • Legal obligations (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR): complying with statutory requirements.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

easy-host uses a server-side session cookie for authentication. No third-party tracking cookies are used. We do not use analytics cookies.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party OAuth providers for authentication. When you log in via an OAuth provider, they may process data according to their own privacy policies. We do not use third-party analytics services.

6. Recipients and Transfers

We do not sell your data. Where we use service providers (e.g., hosting), they act as processors under Art. 28 GDPR and process data only on our instructions. We do not transfer data outside the EU unless adequate safeguards are in place.

7. Retention

  • Account data: retained for the life of your account; deleted upon account deletion.
  • User content (uploaded files): retained until you remove it or delete your account.
  • Server logs and security data: retained for a short period unless needed to investigate issues.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No online service can guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights (EU/EEA)

You have the rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection (Art. 15–21 GDPR). Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw consent at any time with effect for the future. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in Germany.

10. Children

The Platform is not intended for children under 16. Do not create an account if you are under 16.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The "Last Updated" date reflects the effective date of the current version.

12. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact: oglimmer@gmail.com.
